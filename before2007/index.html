About me and this site

Hi! I'm a software engineer born in Oviedo (Spain) but based in Sydney (Australia). I'm currently working for Atlassian. Previously, I was a Semantic Web researcher and I participated in large-scale European research projects as well as in W3C standardisation groups. You can find out more about my research activity and scientific publications. I occasionally enjoy teaching on technical subjects such as XML, Linked Data and Semantic Web.

I'm also a Free Software advocate. I was a founder of AsturLiNUX, a local Linux and Free Software user group at Asturias. Read more about my Free Software activity.
