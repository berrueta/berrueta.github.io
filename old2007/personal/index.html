Small bio

I was born in 1979, and I live in Asturias (Spain). I studied Computer Science at University of Oviedo, and I worked as a system administrator in a couple of sites until I joined CTIC Foundation in 2005. I've been a free software advocate and developer since 1998.

I love the stillness and landscapes of the mountains and I take a hike whenever I can.

Take a look a my photos at Flickr.
