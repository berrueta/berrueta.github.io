I’ve been a reviewer or a member of the Program Committee for these events:

  • 9th International Semantic Web Conference (ISWC 2010), posters and demos. Shanghai, China, Nov 2010.
  • 3rd international workshop Social Data on the Web (SDoW2010). Shanghai, China, 8 Nov 2010.
  • Workshop on Personal Semantic Data on the Desktop and on the Web. Lisbon, Portugal, Oct 2010.
  • 2nd International Workshop on Social Data on the Web (SDoW2009). Washington DC, USA, Oct 2009.
  • Conferencia Ibero-Americana IADIS WWW/Internet 2009. Madrid, Spain, Oct 2009.
  • 1st International Enterprise X.0 Workshop: From Web 2.0 in Enterprises towards a Corporate Web X.0. Poznan (Poland), Apr 2009.
  • PICKME2008 (Personal Identification and Collaborations: Knowledge Mediation and Extraction). Karlsruhe, Germany, Oct 2008.
  • SDoW2008 (Social Data on the Web workshop). Karlsruhe, Germany, Oct 2008.
  • FEWS (Finding Experts on the Web with Semantics) workshop. Busan, Korea, Nov 2007.
  • Jornadas de Software Libre en Asturias. Asturias, 2001-2006.

  • RDFa Primer: Bridging the Human and Data Webs, W3C Editor’s Draft
